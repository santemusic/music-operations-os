# Music Operations OS - 600 Prompt Library

A comprehensive, professional prompt library designed for music backoffice operations. This collection contains 600+ prompts organized into 6 strategic packs covering all major functions of modern music businesses.

## Overview

The Music Operations OS is your AI-powered operations assistant, delivering structured, professional outputs for:
- **Label Operations** - Executive decisions, releases, finance, compliance
- **Artist Management** - Career strategy, content, revenue, wellbeing
- **A&R & Publishing** - Talent scouting, song development, rights management
- **Marketing & DSP Growth** - Campaign strategy, playlisting, paid media
- **Booking & Touring** - Live strategy, routing, logistics
- **Finance, Royalties & Legal** - Cashflow, payments, contracts, compliance

## How to Use

1. **Select your pack** based on your role or task
2. **Find the relevant prompt** by number or title
3. **Copy the prompt template** into your AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
4. **Replace the bracketed placeholder** `[PASTE CONTEXT...]` with your actual data
5. **Run the prompt** and receive a structured, professional output

### Example Workflow

```
Task: I need a daily label brief
→ Open Pack 1 - Executive Operations
→ Find P1-001 - Daily Label Brief
→ Copy the prompt
→ Paste your inbox, priorities, and notes
→ Submit and receive structured brief with key findings, action plan, and next steps
```

## Output Format

All prompts follow a consistent, executive-ready structure:
- **Executive Summary** - High-level overview of key points
- **Key Findings** - Organized facts, assumptions, and gaps
- **Action Plan** - Prioritized steps with owners and deadlines
- **Owners & Deadlines** - Clear accountability matrix
- **Risks** - Potential issues and mitigation
- **Decisions Needed** - Questions requiring leadership input
- **Recommended Next Steps** - Immediate actions

## Prompt Naming Convention

Prompts follow the format: `PXYZ`
- **P** = Pack indicator
- **X** = Pack number (1-6)
- **YZ** = Sequential prompt number (001-100+)

Example: `P1-045` = Pack 1, Prompt 45 (Label Services Evaluation)
